About

The Dermal Archive is a curated catalog of acne-safe, non-comedogenic products — skincare, makeup, body, hair, and tools chosen to support clear, healthy skin without asking you to give up the things you love using.

Every product here is vetted against known pore-clogging ingredients, so you can shop by what's actually safe for your skin instead of decoding labels yourself. Nothing is perfect and skin is personal, but my hope is to take the guesswork out of building a routine that keeps your barrier happy and your breakouts at bay.

How Products Are Vetted*

Non-Comedogenic

Every formula is run through the Acne Clinic NYC Pore-Clogging Ingredients Checker. If a product contains an ingredient known to clog pores, it doesn't make the archive.

  • No flagged comedogenic oils, butters, or waxes
  • Full ingredient list reviewed — not just marketing claims
  • Re-checked when brands reformulate

Barrier-First

A preference for gentle, barrier-supporting formulas — nothing needlessly stripping, and no harsh actives hidden inside everyday basics like cleansers or body wash.

Fragrance & Irritants

  • Fragrance and common irritants are noted so you can decide what your skin tolerates
  • Fragrance-free options prioritized for reactive or sensitized skin
  • Actives called out so you don't accidentally double up

*This is a living list that grows and changes as I test more and learn more. The Dermal Archive is meant to be a calm, honest space for building a routine that actually works for your skin.
