Why I picked this

Reusable silicone patches that hold your eye serum or cream against your skin so it actually absorbs instead of evaporating. Once you've tried this, throwing away a single-use sheet mask feels absurd. The patches are thin, comfortable enough to wear while I do my skincare and brush my teeth, and after about 15 minutes my under-eyes look noticeably more hydrated and less puffy.

Best for

All skin types. Anyone using an eye serum or cream they want to get more out of, anyone tired of the waste from disposable sheet masks. Great for traveling since they pack flat in their tin and weigh nothing.

How I use it

After cleansing, I press in a few drops of an eye serum or cream and wait about twenty seconds for it to absorb. Then the masks go on and stay for 15-20 minutes while I get ready. They peel off easily, I rinse them under cool water, pat dry with a clean towel, and pop them back in the tin. The remaining product on my skin gets pressed in or layered with moisturizer.

One honest note

Placement, consistency, and hygiene are everything. Make sure you apply the masks correctly so they cover the undereye area exactly where you want the serum or cream to sink in. Use a few times a week and make sure you properly clean and sanitize after each use.
