Easy Bake Blurring Setting Spray with 16-Hour Wear

Acne-Safe Verified
Why I picked this

Most setting sprays are alcohol-heavy and dry the skin out by midday but this formula leans on modified rice starch for blur and oil control, mineral clay for shine reduction, and five forms of hyaluronic acid plus niacinamide for hydration. It locks makeup in without flattening or drying the skin underneath flus it's fragrance-free and alcohol-free, which is rare in this category.

Best for

All skin types, but especially combination, oily, or breakout-prone skin where traditional setting sprays cause irritation. Also genuinely useful for dry skin because the hyaluronic acid keeps it from feeling tight. Helpful in humidity or on any day you need makeup to last past lunch.

How I use it

At the end of my makeup routine. Spray generously and let it dry.

One honest note

It's a blurring spray, not a dewy-finish spray. If your skin already runs dry or you want a glassy, lit-from-within finish, this will read more matte so pair it with a hydrating mist on top, or reach for a different formula entirely. The nozzle can also clog if you don't store it upright.
