The Ordinary

Squalane Cleanser

$21.00
Acne-Safe Verified
Why I picked this

This balm-to-oil-to-milk format starts as a solid, melts on contact with skin into an oil that dissolves makeup, then emulsifies with water into a milky cleanser that rinses clean. The result is skin that feels actually clean rather than tight, with the moisture barrier intact. It removes everything from sunscreen to long-wear makeup without an aggressive surfactant, and all at only $10.

Best for

All skin types. Also great for the first step of a double cleanse.

How I use it

Onto dry skin and hands scoop a small amount and massage across your face. Add water to emulsify into a milky texture, then rinse with cool water. For a deep clean, follow with a water-based cleanser

One honest note

It's a balm-to-oil cleanser, so it doesn't foam or feel traditionally squeaky-clean. If that's what you want from a cleanser, follow with a traditional water-based one like the one from Vanicream or Dr.Idriss.
