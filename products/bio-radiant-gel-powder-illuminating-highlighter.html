Makeup
HAUS LABS

Bio-Radiant Gel-Powder Illuminating Highlighter

$40.00
Acne-Safe Verified
Why I picked this

A highlighter that behaves like a cream and sets like a powder, without the chunky glitter. The gel-powder hybrid formula melts onto skin and gives a soft-focus glow rather than a hard strobe — flattering in person and in photos. It's also talc-free and infused with fermented arnica and squalane. Haus Labs is one of the few clean makeup brands that publishes its non-comedogenic standards, which matters when you're putting the products directly on your face every day.

Best for

All skin types & tones. There are ten shades from cool pearl to deep bronze. Especially good for mature skin or anyone with texture, since the blurring effect smooths rather than emphasizes. Sensitive eyes can use it on the lid because there's no talc.

How I use it

A light sweep on the high points of the cheekbones, the bridge of the nose, the cupid's bow, and the inner corners of the eyes (or all over the lid). I apply with a fluffy brush for everyday and tap it on with a fingertip when I want a more concentrated, dewy effect on top of cream blush. It builds well, so go light and add as needed.

One honest note

At $40 for a highlighter, it's at the higher end of the category. The pan size is generous and the formula goes a long way, so cost-per-use lands reasonably, but if you only wear highlighter occasionally, a cheaper option will serve you fine. If you want a "blinding" highlight right out the gate, this isn't that. It's a glow, not a flash.
