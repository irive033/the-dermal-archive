Why I picked this

A lightweight cream concealer that delivers medium coverage over dark circles, redness, and blemishes, and then sets to a natural-radiant finish that wears for hours without creasing, caking, or going greasy. The formula is built around niacinamide and hyaluronic acid, so it's also doing skincare work underneath the makeup.

Best for

All skin types.

How I use it

Apply to the back of my hand and then blend into a concealer brush before applying underneath my eyes and anywhere else I need coverage.

One honest note

Niacinimade can be slightly irritating on some skin types, so if you decide to try this concealer, add it into your routine when you aren't trying out any other new products, and look out for breakouts, or itching/redness.
