Minimal SPF Makeup Routine

Isabella Rivera · May 17, 2026

All good makeup starts with skincare. A streamlined cosmetics approach that prioritizes skin health while maintaining a polished look.

Foundation

Start with a foundation that includes SPF for everyday protection. Layer with concealer for targeted coverage where needed.

Concealer

Bronzer

Add dimension with cream and powder bronzers. Blend for a natural, sun-kissed effect.

Blush

Apply blush in both liquid and powder formulations for a layered, long-lasting flush of color.

Setting Powder

Set everything in place with a light powder to ensure longevity throughout the day.

Lips

Finish with lip liner, lipstick, and a hydrating balm for a polished but natural lip.

Optional

Setting spray and mascara for finishing touches when you want a little extra.

Featured brands: Rare Beauty, Ciele, Westman Atelier, Saie, Charlotte Tilbury
